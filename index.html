Mirek Stolee

Game creator, scholar, and instructor

Hello, folks! I'm Mirek. I'm a Texts & Technology (T&T) Ph.D. Student at the University of Central Florida. I do research about the intersection between digital and analog media, currently focusing the history of escape games including live-action escape rooms. Before joining the T&T program, I was a lecturer in the Art & Design: Games & Playable Media and Computational Media departments at UCSC. The classes I taught covered game history, analog and digital game design, and 3D modeling. I also earned my Master's Degree in Computational Media from UCSC.
