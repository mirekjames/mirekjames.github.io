Mirek Stolee

Game creator, scholar, and instructor

Hello, folks! I'm Mirek. I graduated from the University of Notre Dame with a degree in Marketing and Television Studies. After a year of teaching programming at the South Bend Code School, I pursued my master's degree in Computational Media at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC). I do research about the intersection between digital and analog media, currently focusing on escape rooms and board game adaptations. I'm currently a lecturer in the Art & Design: Games & Playable Media program at UCSC.
